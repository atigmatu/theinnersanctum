I haven't truly cried in a while.

I've shed a tear or two, been emotional and all that,
but let everything out?
Cried so hard and long that
that sleepy warmth comes to live behind
my eyelids and seeps into the depths of
soul, leaving a heavy but familiar weight?

No, I haven't cried in some time.

And I don't know if thats a bad thing or not.
I've been sad, been heartbroken, I've been
losing all the things I used to care the most about.



I need to cry for a while.
