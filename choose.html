Have you ever gone to a second-hand store and
they have a big box of old photos from who knows where
and who knows who took them?

It's the strangest experience to go through them,
You start to recognize these people you've
never known and never will know;
see snippets of their lives.

After a while you'll get a headache.
Whether that's from the concentration
or meeting all these new people,
either way you'll feel groggy.

I sometimes wonder what I would do if I found myself
in between grandma's 90th birthday and
a winning baseball game.

Would I fit?

does it truly matter?
