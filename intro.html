Welcome to the inner sanctum!

This is a website/experience based on healing
trauma and the prepetual human condition known
as remembering that you are a person.
(as pretentious as that sounds)
Keeping that in mind, there may be a few instances
in which you become overwhelmed.

This experience is not meant to frighten, it is
a dive into the struggle I have and you may also;
to stay present and continually grow.

If the time comes in which you feel overwhelmed,
I urge you to simply close out, no progress can be
made when things feel like they are too much,
and I'm not going anywhere,
I promise.

There will be moments in which the way forward (or back) may seem impossible, take a deep breath, evaluate where you are and explore.
You're never as lost as you think you are

care to continue onwards?
